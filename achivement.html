Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to SunshuneAcademy! We understand that you may have some questions about our CBSE tuition programs, teaching methods, and enrollment process. Find answers to commonly asked queries below.

1. What classes and subjects do you offer at SunshuneAcademy?

We offer CBSE tuition for classes 6 to 12 covering core subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Science. For senior secondary students, we also provide specialized coaching in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Accountancy.

2. What is the teaching methodology followed at SunshuneAcademy?

Our experienced faculty uses concept-based teaching, regular assessments, personalized doubt-clearing sessions, and NCERT-based practice to strengthen students' fundamentals and improve academic performance.

3. Do you offer any crash courses or exam preparation batches?

Yes! We conduct special crash courses and revision batches before the board exams and term exams. These sessions focus on important topics, previous year question papers, and time management strategies.

4. How can I enroll my child at SunshuneAcademy?

You can visit our tuition center or register online through our website. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process, fee structure, and batch timings to find the best fit for your childâ€™s academic needs.

5. Do you provide progress reports or feedback to parents?

Absolutely! We believe in keeping parents informed. Regular progress reports, periodic parent-teacher meetings, and performance analysis are provided to ensure transparency and track the student's improvement.
